## English Version

### Introduction

Welcome to "Let's Save the Earth" ("the Game"), a climate simulation game developed by Nasbarok ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By using the Game, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

### Information We Collect

#### 1. Game Data (Stored Locally)
- Game progress and save states
- Game settings and preferences
- Language preferences

**This data is stored locally on your device using localStorage and is NOT transmitted to our servers.**

#### 2. Analytics and Advertising Data
We use third-party services that may collect information:

**Google AdMob (Advertising)**
- Device identifiers
- IP address (anonymized)
- Ad interaction data
- General location (country/region level)

For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

**Google Gemini AI (Event Descriptions)**
- The Game uses Google Gemini AI to generate dynamic event descriptions
- No personal data is sent to Gemini - only game context for content generation
- See [Google AI Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

#### 3. In-App Purchases
If you make in-app purchases (AI Boost feature):
- Transaction data is processed by Google Play
- We do not store payment information
- Purchase history is managed by Google Play

### How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the Game
- Display relevant advertisements
- Process in-app purchases
- Generate dynamic game content via AI
- Improve game performance and user experience

### Data Retention

- **Local game data**: Stored until you uninstall the Game or clear app data
- **Analytics data**: Retained by third-party providers according to their policies
- **Purchase data**: Managed by Google Play

### Children's Privacy

The Game is rated PEGI 7 and is suitable for ages 7+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The Game:
- Does not require account creation
- Does not collect names, emails, or personal identifiers
- Uses contextual advertising only (non-personalized for users who opt out)

Parents can disable personalized ads through device settings.

### Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View your locally stored game data
- **Delete**: Clear game data by uninstalling or clearing app storage
- **Opt-out**: Disable personalized ads in your device settings
- **Withdraw consent**: Stop using the Game at any time

### Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Google AdMob | Advertising | [Link](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| Google Play Billing | In-App Purchases | [Link](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| Google Gemini AI | Content Generation | [Link](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |

### Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the Game and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

### Introduction

Bienvenue dans "Sauvons la Terre" ("le Jeu"), un jeu de simulation climatique developpe par Nasbarok ("nous" ou "notre"). Cette Politique de Confidentialite explique comment nous collectons, utilisons et protegeons vos informations lorsque vous utilisez notre application mobile.

### Informations que nous collectons

#### 1. Donnees de jeu (stockees localement)
- Progression et sauvegardes de jeu
- Parametres et preferences du jeu
- Preferences de langue

**Ces donnees sont stockees localement sur votre appareil via localStorage et NE SONT PAS transmises a nos serveurs.**

#### 2. Donnees d'analyse et de publicite
Nous utilisons des services tiers qui peuvent collecter des informations:

**Google AdMob (Publicite)**
- Identifiants d'appareil
- Adresse IP (anonymisee)
- Donnees d'interaction publicitaire
- Localisation generale (niveau pays/region)

- Le Jeu utilise Google Gemini AI pour generer des descriptions d'evenements dynamiques
- Aucune donnee personnelle n'est envoyee a Gemini - uniquement le contexte de jeu pour la generation de contenu
- Voir la [Politique de confidentialite Google AI](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

#### 3. Achats integres
Si vous effectuez des achats integres (fonction Boost IA):
- Les donnees de transaction sont traitees par Google Play
- Nous ne stockons pas d'informations de paiement
- L'historique des achats est gere par Google Play

### Comment nous utilisons vos informations

Nous utilisons les informations collectees pour:
- Fournir et maintenir le Jeu
- Afficher des publicites pertinentes
- Traiter les achats integres
- Generer du contenu de jeu dynamique via l'IA
- Ameliorer les performances et l'experience utilisateur

### Conservation des donnees

- **Donnees de jeu locales**: Stockees jusqu'a la desinstallation ou l'effacement des donnees

- **Donnees d'achat**: Gerees par Google Play

### Confidentialite des enfants

Le Jeu est classe PEGI 7 et convient aux 7 ans et plus. Nous ne collectons pas sciemment d'informations personnelles aupres d'enfants de moins de 13 ans. Le Jeu:
- Ne necessite pas de creation de compte
- Ne collecte pas de noms, emails ou identifiants personnels
- Utilise uniquement la publicite contextuelle (non personnalisee pour les utilisateurs qui refusent)

Les parents peuvent desactiver les publicites personnalisees via les parametres de l'appareil.

### Vos droits

Vous avez le droit de:
- **Acceder**: Consulter vos donnees de jeu stockees localement
- **Supprimer**: Effacer les donnees en desinstallant ou en vidant le stockage
- **Refuser**: Desactiver les publicites personnalisees dans les parametres
